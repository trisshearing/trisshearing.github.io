Safe Routes to Schools

×

A map to help families find safe routes to school on foot, bike, scooter or wheelchair

Layers

Use the menu on the right of the map to choose your backdrop. Greyscale is best for viewing the Routes and Restrictions overlays, Satellite gives an aerial view, Public Transport displays bus and rail connections.

Schools

Start by finding your school on the map, hover over each school to see the name. Clicking on the school icon shows the approximate travel times to that school, hover over for detail. Click the icon again to remove. Only works for Dulwich Village Infants School in prototype.

Quiet routes

In the menu on the right of the map, select "Routes" to display the colour overlays, classifying roads by traffic levels and suitability for active travel. These are based on experience of SRS members and not on any form of traffic monitoring. Hover over each route for more information.

Restrictions

Select "Restrictions" to display measures to reduce traffic flow (modal filters and bus gates) including COVID-19 experimental traffic orders which may be subject to change, alongside more established features. Hover over the icon for more details, including times and direction of restrictions.
